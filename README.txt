Take control of your style maintenance in Oxygen Builder.

== Description ==

Style Flow is one of the most popular extensions in Editor Enhancer! Because of its popularity, it has also been released singularly, though it is still part of the active EE premium plugin.

For more information, visit https://editorenhancer.com/docs/style-flow.

To upgrade from just Style Flow to the entire Editor Enhancer suite, visit your account at https://editorenhancer.com/login > Purchase History and upgrade.

== Installation ==

1. Click on the download link in your purchase confirmation email if you have not already downloaded it after your purchase.
2. Download the plugin's zip file.
3. Go to Plugins > Add New in your WordPress admin. Click "Add New" button, then "Upload Plugin" button, then "Choose File", browse to and select the plugin's zip file.
4. Activate the plugin.
5. Enter the license key and activate your plugin license at WP Admin > Oxygen > Style Flow.

Valid license key should be entered for the plugin to function and to receive automatic updates.
